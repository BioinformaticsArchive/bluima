Figure 9. Hypoplastic and asymmetric olfactory bulbs with lamination defects in adult
Shep1 knock-out mice. A, Adult brains illustrating an asymmetrical olfactory bulb malformation
in the Shep1 knock-out. Arrows point to the olfactory bulbs. Scale bar, 5 mm. B, Coronal sections
of wild-type (WT) and Shep1 knock-out (KO) olfactory bulbs double labeled with Nissl green and
anti-olfactory marker protein (OMP) antibody. Sections from the more affected olfactory bulb of
the knock-out are shown. Scale bar, 500 m.
