PPE Extract Preparation
PPE (300 g) was obtained from the Oriental Medical Hospital (Dongguk University College of Oriental Medicine) and extracted with boiling water for
3 h. Extracts were evaporated under reduced pressure by 75%, 85%, and 95% ethanol solution. Then
extracts were lyophilized and stored as specimen no.
P-65 at the Oriental Medical Hospital. The last
extracts were diluted by 0.9% NaCl and filtrated.
The extract solution was stored at 4Â°C, according to
the method of Fang (1986). [3H]ATC iodide was purchased from New England Nuclear (Boston, MA).
All other chemicals were commercial products of
reagent grade.
