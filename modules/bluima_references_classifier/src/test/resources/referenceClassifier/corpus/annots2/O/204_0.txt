Apparently animals had an existing set of expectations derived
from their previous experience in the familiar task. When
confronted with a novel instruction, they could draw on these
expectations to test the novel stimulus and later adapt their

or “erroneous” behavioral performance thus would reflect in-appropriate but otherwise unchanged expectations evoked by
