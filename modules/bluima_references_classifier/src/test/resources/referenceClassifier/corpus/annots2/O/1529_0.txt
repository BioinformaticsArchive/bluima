Entry criteria: criteria for entry to the study would include
Term infants 36 wk of age
Perinatal depression
Clinical plus EEG criteria at 6 h for moderate or severe encephalopathy
Intervention: hypothermia would be the first therapy used; the study would
include the following 2 groups
Hypothermia
Hypothermia plus (possibilities include earlier onset of hypothermia;
prolonged duration of study; lower temperature; systemic vs selective
method of cooling; other drugs such as phenobarbital, magnesium, and
allopurinol)
Risk factors: blood would be saved to examine for the following potential risk
factors
Male vs female
Genetic polymorphisms
Coagulation profile
Follow-up at 18 mo
